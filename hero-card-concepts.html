Notes
A — Warm Coal
Most grounded. Matches the app's warm charcoal palette almost exactly but with a richer lift. Subtle and premium — the hero card recedes into the screen, making the number pop.
B — Ember Glow
Most on-brand. The red-brown warmth directly evokes glowing coals — ties closely to the "Ember" name. The glow circle amplifies the fireside feel without being distracting.
C — Ashen Gold
Best contrast with the accent. The golden radial glow mirrors the flame icon and amber accent color, creating a subtle halo effect. Neutral enough to not fight the content.
Current hero card uses a blue-tinted gradient (0.085–0.145 blue channel) — all three options above replace that with warm tones that align with the ember surface palette (0.059–0.078 bg, 0.136 surfaceBase).
