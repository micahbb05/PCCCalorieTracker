The takeaway
The warm palette does real work here — swapping cold #121825 → #0F0D0A and neon blue #4F80FF → #C4965A makes the same layout feel noticeably more considered. The green / yellow / red macro bars disappearing is the biggest single win: replacing them with teal, amber, and clay removes the "generic fitness dashboard" signal entirely.

But the structural issues remain. The thick 8px progress bars still feel blunt. The floating amber + tab is calmer than blue but still disrupts the rhythm. The chunky rounded cards still tile awkwardly. The bold fire emoji still reads as templated. Color gets you 40% of the way — the other 60% is structure and typographic hierarchy.
