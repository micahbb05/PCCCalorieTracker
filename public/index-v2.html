Everything included

Powerful features.
Zero friction.

Every tool you need to understand your nutrition — nothing you don't.

01
Campus Dining Menus
Live integration with PCC Dining pulls menus from all locations every night. Tap any item to log it instantly with verified calories and macros.
02
AI Photo Logging
Snap a photo of your plate. Gemini Vision identifies each item, estimates portions, and pre-fills your log — cross-referencing today's actual menu when available.
03
Apple Health Sync
Step count and active energy from Apple Health dynamically adjusts your daily calorie goal so your target always reflects what your body actually burned.
04
Barcode Scanning
Scan any packaged product barcode for instant, verified nutrition data from OpenFoodFacts and the USDA database — no searching required.
05
History & Trends
Review net calorie trends and meal distribution charts spanning 7 days to 2 full years. A calendar view lets you drill into any past day at a glance.
06
Private by Default
Every entry lives on your device. Enable optional iCloud CloudKit sync for seamless continuity across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac — all end-to-end encrypted.

Campus Integration

Your dining hall,
already inside

PCC Dining menus from all three locations are cached every midnight so they're ready before breakfast. No manual entry — just tap what you're eating.

  • Four Winds, Varsity & Grab N Go
  • Refreshed automatically at midnight
  • Search across all locations at once
  • AI plate estimation from plate photos

Daily Dashboard

Goals that adapt
to how you move

Your calorie target isn't static. Apple Health feeds in your step count and active energy each day so your goal adjusts automatically — rewarding activity without manual math.

  • Calories remaining, consumed & goal
  • Protein, carbs & fat with progress bars
  • Active energy from Apple Health
  • Home screen & lock screen widgets

Long-term Insights

Seven days
to two years

Rich historical charts reveal trends you'd never catch day to day — net calorie averages, meal distribution patterns, and rolling time-range comparisons up to 2 full years of data.

  • Flexible range selector: 7d → 2yr
  • Meal distribution pie charts
  • Calendar view with daily drilldown
  • Net vs. intake trend lines
