PCC Dining menus built in. Track calories with AI plate estimates.

Connect Pensacola Christian College meal menus, take a photo of your plate for AI portion estimates, and combine it with Apple Health, barcode scanning, and USDA food search — all in one app.

PCC Dining Integration

Calorie Tracker connects directly to Pensacola Christian College Dining via Nutrislice. View today’s menus with calories and protein for every item, then add items to your log with a single tap. New: snap a photo of your plate and let AI estimate portions for what’s actually on your tray, then fine-tune with simple sliders before logging.

  • Four Winds — Lunch & dinner
  • Varsity — Breakfast, lunch & dinner
  • Grab N Go — Breakfast, lunch & dinner

The app automatically shows the right meal (breakfast, lunch, or dinner) based on the current time. Menus update daily so you always see what’s available today.

More ways to log food

Health-based goals

Connect Apple Health for height, weight, sex, age, and activity. The app calculates your BMR and adjusts your daily calorie goal based on your burn.

AI plate photo estimates

Choose items from the PCC menu, take a photo of your plate, and let AI estimate how much of each item is actually there. You stay in control with per-item sliders and a setting to let AI refine base servings or stick strictly to the menu sizes.

Barcode scanning

Scan product barcodes to look up nutrition from Open Food Facts. Add packaged foods in seconds.

USDA food search

Search USDA FoodData Central for thousands of foods. Get detailed nutrition info for fresh ingredients and common items.

Manual & quick-add

Enter calories and nutrients manually, or save foods as quick-adds for fast logging.
