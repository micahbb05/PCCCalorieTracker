Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

Calorie Tracker ("the App") is designed with privacy in mind. This policy describes what data the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. The App complies with Apple's App Store privacy requirements and best practices.

1. Data Stored on Your Device

All personal data stays on your device:

  • Meal entries (food names, calories, nutrients)
  • Your calorie deficit goal and nutrient targets
  • Quick-add foods
  • Daily calorie and burned-calorie history
  • Onboarding and preference choices

This data is stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2. Health Data (Apple Health)

If you connect Apple Health, the App reads the following data only on your device:

  • Biological sex — for BMR calculation
  • Date of birth — to calculate age for BMR
  • Height — for BMR calculation
  • Body mass (weight) — for BMR calculation
  • Active energy burned — to adjust your daily calorie goal based on activity

The App does not write any data to Apple Health. Health data is used solely to personalize your BMR and daily calorie goal. It is never sent to our servers or shared.

3. Motion & Fitness (Step Count)

With your permission, the App may read your step count from the device's pedometer to estimate activity burn and adjust your daily calorie goal. Step data stays on your device and is not transmitted.

4. Camera

When you use barcode scanning, the camera is used to read barcodes. Images are processed on-device; no photos or video are stored or transmitted.

5. Data Sent to External Services

The App makes limited network requests for features:

  • USDA Food Search — When you search for foods, your search query is sent to our backend, which proxies the request to USDA FoodData Central. No account or identifying information is sent.
  • Open Food Facts — When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts to retrieve nutrition data. No other data is included.
  • Firebase — The App reads PCC Dining menu data synced to Firestore. No user data is written or linked to this.

6. Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking. We do not collect or sell your data.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, please contact us through the App Store listing or your preferred method.
